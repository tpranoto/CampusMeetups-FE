Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions:

  • How do I create a trip? You can create a trip by clicking on the 'Start a Trip' button on the homepage.
  • How do I join a trip? Browse through the available trips and click 'Join' on your desired trip.
  • How can I cancel my trip? To cancel your trip, go to your trip details and select 'Cancel'.
